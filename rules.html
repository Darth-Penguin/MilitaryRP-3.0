Triflux Gaming Rules
Rules last updated on: February 1, 2016

Updated:
- Rules of Role Play(Name Rule)

This gamemode is NOT pure Team Deathmatch. The purpose of this game mode is to emulate realistic military situations and conflicts.

Players are NOT allowed under the map under any circumstances. If found under the map you will be banned for one week, if you are found with printers it will be a month. If you happen to fall through just type "kill" in console.

3.0 Rules
  1. Do not build on or around other bases.
  2. Factions must base at their own base.
    - Factions can only print at their base.
  3. War reasons must have a valid point. "Oil" is not a reason.
  4. If you are at your faction's base and you die during an enemy raid, do not go back to kill the raiders for 5 minutes.
  5. Do not spawn trap/kill people in their bases/spawns.
  6. There are now "war crimes". If a faction does one of the following things, the United Nations may take action.
    • Illegally killing people from another faction.
    • Calling war on all for world domination.
    • Teaming with anarchists.
  7. Abductors may only abduct someone every 5 minutes.
  8. Raids may only last 10 minutes after you advert raid.
  9. Do not randomly handcuff or grapple people.
  10. Scavengers (and other equivalents) may counter raid a base that is being raided. You must do "/advert Counter Raid!"
  11. When abducting/kidnaping someone, you must do "/advert Abduction!".
    • The max amount of money you can demand for a hostage is $50,000.
    • You may only abduct one person at a time.
    • Abductions may only last for 15 minutes after you advert. After that, you have to either kill them or let them go.
    • If you are abducted, you may not rejoin the server to avoid rp.
General Rules
  1. Shooting/killing in safezone is not permitted for any reason. If you shoot someone when you are outside of the safe zone, you are not permitted to reenter the safe zone for 30 seconds.
  2. No Racist / Sexist / Homophobic comments or names.
  3. Do not random death match (RDM). You must declare war to kill a member of another faction.
  4. Do not break new life rule (NLR). NLR applies to everyone. If you were raiding, do not return to the area you were killed at for atleast 5 minutes.
  5. Do not abuse your props. For example:
    • prop blocking
    • prop climbing
    • prop spamming
    • prop surfing
    • prop killing
    Cars are considered props.
  6. Stealing is not permitted in SZ for any reasons, with the exception of printers.
  7. Running someone over in a car is classified as Car Deathmatch, a kind of RDM. This must be avoided at all costs. It is the responsibility of the driver to avoid players and the responsibility of players to avoid cars. There is no gray area; however, involuntary CDM, meaning CDM that occurred out of the control of either party, is automatically the responsibility of the driver.

  9. Keypads cannot be used to raid, you must use keypad crackers. If a friend types in the code or someone remembers it from being with that team, it is NLR and FailRP.
  10. You may not make a double fading door (2 fading doors on top of each other which traps the person trying to unlock the second door inside it, or in any other way unable to break into the base).

  12. Do not impersonate staff, this will get you a lengthy ban.

Rules of Role Play

This applies to all Military Factions (USMC, Cuban, Russian, German, Etc.) :

  1. All players must follow their commanding officer's orders (person ranked higher than you)
  2. All players must state when they are going to war with another faction before killing them; if you don't it's considered RDM. (Ex: /Advert: USMC Declares war on Cuba) YOU MUST PUT A REALISTIC RP REASON. You can be demoted or kicked if you do not comply. Some realistic examples are as follows:
    • War on (FACTION) due to the attack on our troops.
    • War on (FACTION) due to refusal to engage in peaceful negotiations
    • War on (FACTION) in order to weaken their position of domination.
    • War on (FACTION) in order to infiltrate their base and claim their money/printers and weapons.
    • War on (FACTION) in order to defend the troops/property of (ALLY).

  4. Military Personnel of the same faction may NOT have multiple bases.(Example: Cubans build a base near spawn and one in the back of the map)
  5. If a player disobeys a truce between two factions they may be demoted.
  6. Military Personnel are not permitted to fire on civilians unless fired on first.
  7. All players must follow their commanding officer's orders. If not, the player is subject to kick/demotion.

Faction Specific Rules

Mercenary Rules
  1. Mercenaries are allowed to offensively attack another faction only if paid by a MILITARY faction. Military factions cannot pay them to attack civilians. A mercenary may only kill a scavenger if said scavenger is raiding their employer.
  2. Mercenaries are allowed to accept money for attacking an opposing faction, providing protection, etc.
    • Minimum Price: $500
    • Maximum Price: $1,000,000
    Each payment only lasts for one life and cannot be used to hire a mercenary for multiple lives. If there is a C.E.L.L Commander online and someone pays the Commander directly, your contract lasts until the Commander dies, or declares the contract as complete/over.
  3. Mercenaries are not hitmen. They are not allowed to be hired to kill single people.
  4. Mercenaries are allowed to team up with factions if paid by that faction.
  5. Mercenaries are NOT allowed to be paid by civilians unless it's for protection.
  6. Mercenaries must advert each time they are hired, but do not have to disclose their employer if the employer has specifically asked them to do so.
  7. Mercenaries must advert that they are unhired or that their contract has ended upon their death.

Civilian and Scavenger Faction:

  1. Civilians are allowed to own weapons for self defense purposes only.
  2. Civilians are not allowed to raid, with or without factions.
  3. Scavengers are allowed to kill when raiding but must first advert raid. (Example: /advert Raiding!)
  4. Scavengers are permitted to raid any location.
  5. Scavengers are also subject to all NLR rules.
  6. Scavengers may NOT abduct/kidnap people.
